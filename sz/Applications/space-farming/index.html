About Space Farming
Space Farming

Casual farm simulation on a space station. Grow 7 crop types through growth stages, tend 4 livestock types, survive space weather events (solar flares boost growth, meteor showers damage crops), and sell produce for credits. Features crop-growth sparkle effects, harvest particle bursts, and weather visual overlays.

Part of the »SynthelicZ« Desktop.
