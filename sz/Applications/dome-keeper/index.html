About Dome Keeper
Dome Keeper

Hybrid mine-and-defend game. Protect your dome from alien waves on the surface while mining iron, water, and cobalt underground. Upgrade your weapon, dome armor, drill speed, fire rate, and carry capacity to survive increasingly difficult waves. Features laser glow effects, mining debris particles, dome-hit screen shake, and smooth surface/underground transitions.

Part of the »SynthelicZ« Desktop.
