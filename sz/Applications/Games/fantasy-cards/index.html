How to Play
Objective
Reduce your opponent's HP to 0 before they do the same to you. Both players start with 50 HP.

Turns & Mana
Each turn you get mana to play cards. Your max mana increases by 1 each turn (up to 10). Each card costs mana shown in the blue gem (top-left of card). Play as many cards as your mana allows, then click End Turn or press E.

Card Types
⚔ AttackDeal damage to the enemy (absorbed by shield first)
🛡 DefendGain shield points that absorb incoming damage
❤ HealRestore your HP (cannot exceed max HP)
⬆ BuffBoost your attack and defense power for future plays
⬇ DebuffStrip the enemy's accumulated attack and defense buffs
Card Stats
Blue gem (top-left) = Mana cost  •  Colored gem (top-right) = Power value
The power number is the base effect: damage dealt, shield gained, HP restored, or buff/debuff strength.

Combo Synergies
Cards belong to an element: Fire, Storm, Frost, Shadow, Holy, Nature, or Arcane. If you play a card whose element matches another card already played this turn, you get +2 bonus power! Plan your plays to chain same-element cards together.

Deck Building
Press D on the main menu to open the Deck Builder. Build a 30-card deck from 55 unique cards. A good deck balances attack power, defense, healing, and utility. Include cards of the same element for combo potential.

Tips
• Hover over cards to see detailed stats and descriptions
• Cards you cannot afford are dimmed
• Use number keys 1-9 to quickly play cards by position
• Watch the opponent's shield before attacking — sometimes a debuff is more efficient
