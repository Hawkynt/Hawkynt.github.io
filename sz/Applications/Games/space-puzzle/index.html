About Space Puzzle
Space Puzzle

A gravity-manipulation puzzle game where you drag and launch a probe through gravity wells and asteroid fields to reach repair goals. Navigate 12 handcrafted levels of increasing complexity, earn star ratings based on your moves and time, and repair your spacecraft one component at a time.

Part of the »SynthelicZ« Desktop.
