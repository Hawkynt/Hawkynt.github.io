About Tower Defense
Tower Defense

Place 9 tower types (arrow, cannon, frost, lightning, laser, poison, tesla, mortar, flame) along enemy paths to defend your base. Upgrade towers through 3 tiers, manage your gold economy, and survive escalating waves of normal, fast, armored, flying, healer, swarm, shield, and boss enemies across 12 unique maps.

Features auto-wave mode, full mouse-only controls with right-click context menus, pre-wave warnings, and path visualization with spawn/exit markers.

Part of the »SynthelicZ« Desktop.
