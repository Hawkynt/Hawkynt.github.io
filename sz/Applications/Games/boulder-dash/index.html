Boulder Dash - Free Online Cave Exploration Puzzle Game

Play Boulder Dash free in your browser -- dig through underground caves, collect gems, avoid falling boulders and enemies, and reach the exit before time runs out in this classic puzzle-action game.

Features

  • Grid-based cave with walls, dirt, boulders, gems, and an exit
  • Dirt digging and smooth grid movement with lerp interpolation
  • Boulder physics -- boulders fall when unsupported and slide off rounded objects
  • Push boulders horizontally into empty spaces
  • Gem collection with sparkle particle effects and floating score text
  • Gem quota per level to unlock the exit
  • Enemy AI with wall-following movement patterns
  • Level progression with procedural cave generation and increasing difficulty
  • Time limit per level with countdown timer and time bonus scoring
  • Screen shake, gem glow effects, and level-complete confetti
  • High score persistence and tutorial overlay
