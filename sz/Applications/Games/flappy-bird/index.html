Flappy Bird - Free Online Browser Game with Medals and Progressive Difficulty

Tap to flap and guide a bird through an endless series of pipes. Simple one-button controls with progressive difficulty, medal tiers, parallax scrolling backgrounds, and particle effects.

Features

  • One-button gameplay: tap Space or click to flap with gravity physics
  • Progressive difficulty every 12 points: shrinking pipe gaps, faster scrolling, tighter spacing
  • Medal system: Bronze (10+), Silver (25+), Gold (50+), Platinum (100+)
  • Parallax scrolling scenery with clouds, hills, and sky gradient
  • Flap particles, golden score sparkles, floating +1 text, and screen shake on death
  • Death tumble animation before game-over screen
  • Persistent top-5 high scores via localStorage
  • Instant restart from death screen
