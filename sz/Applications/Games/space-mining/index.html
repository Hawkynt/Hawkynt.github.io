About Space Mining
Space Mining

Pilot your mining rig through asteroid fields, drill for precious ores, and sell them at the station. Upgrade your drill speed, cargo capacity, hull armor, engines, and scanner to mine deeper and earn more credits.

Features 4 ore types (iron, gold, platinum, crystal), dynamic market prices, 5 equipment upgrades purchasable at the station shop, floating powerups (shield, speed boost, ore magnet, bonus ore, repair kit), animated parallax starfield with nebulae and shooting stars, scanner overlays, and contextual mining tutorial.

Part of the »SynthelicZ« Desktop.
