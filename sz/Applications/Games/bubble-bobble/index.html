Bubble Bobble - Free Online Platformer Arcade Game

Play Bubble Bobble free in your browser -- shoot bubbles to trap enemies, pop them for points, and collect fruit items for bonus score. Features platform physics with screen wrapping, power-ups, and increasing difficulty across procedurally generated levels.

Features

  • Single-screen platformer with bubble shooting mechanics
  • Trap enemies in bubbles then pop them for points
  • Fruit items spawn from popped enemies for bonus scoring
  • Platform physics with gravity, jumping, and one-way collision detection
  • Screen wrapping -- fall through the bottom and reappear at the top
  • 3 power-up types: Speed boost, Range extension, and Fire
  • Bubbles can be used as temporary platforms
  • Level progression with wind flow, faster enemies, and shorter bubble lifespans
  • Particle effects: bubble pop bursts, trap glow, enemy escape effects, confetti
  • Background sparkle dots, colored brick-pattern platforms, and screen shake
  • High score persistence and tutorial overlay
