Dodge obstacles by switching lanes, jumping and sliding while collecting coins and power-ups in an ever-accelerating run. How far can you go?

Endless Runner - Free Online Auto-Runner Arcade Game

Play Endless Runner free in your browser -- sprint through procedurally generated obstacle courses across three lanes, dodge barriers by switching lanes, jumping, and sliding. Collect coins and power-ups as speed ramps up through day, sunset, night, and neon visual themes.

Features

  • 3-lane auto-runner with procedurally generated obstacle courses
  • Lane switching, jumping, and sliding to dodge obstacles
  • 3 obstacle types: low barriers (jumpable), high barriers (slideable), full blocks (lane change)
  • Coins worth 10 points each with sparkle effects and floating text
  • 3 power-ups: Magnet (attracts coins), Shield (absorbs one hit), Score Multiplier (2x points)
  • Speed acceleration from 8 m/s to 24 m/s with progressive difficulty
  • 4 visual theme tiers: Day, Sunset, Night, Neon -- shifting as speed increases
  • Speed-line effects at high velocities
  • Parallax-scrolling road with lane markings and skyline
  • Screen shake on death, obstacle warning glow, and dust puff effects
  • Touch and mouse input support alongside keyboard controls
  • High score persistence and tutorial overlay
