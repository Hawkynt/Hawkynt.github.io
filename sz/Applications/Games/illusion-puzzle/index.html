Optical Illusion Puzzle - Free Isometric Impossible Geometry Brain Game

Navigate 16 surreal isometric levels featuring Penrose stairs and paradox bridges inspired by Monument Valley and M.C. Escher. Rotate perspective to create and break paths, collect gems, and reach the goal in as few moves as possible.

Features

  • 16 levels of impossible geometry with Penrose stairs and illusion links
  • Perspective rotation through 4 angles to activate hidden paths and teleports
  • Collectible gems with golden sparkle particle effects
  • Isometric diamond-tile grid with raised platforms and 3D depth rendering
  • Glowing path trail highlighting visited tiles
  • Smooth morph animations with smoothstep easing during perspective shifts
  • Warp teleportation with purple burst particles and screen shake
  • Persistent level progress and top-10 high scores ranked by fewest moves
