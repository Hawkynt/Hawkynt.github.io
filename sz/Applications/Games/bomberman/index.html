About Bomberman
Bomberman

Navigate grid-based arenas, place bombs to destroy walls and defeat enemies, and collect 9 types of power-ups to enhance your abilities: Bomb Up, Fire Up, Speed Up, Kick, Remote, Power Kick, Fly, Glove, and Climb. Features 7 enemy types introduced across levels: wandering slow/normal/fast enemies, wall-climbing Climbers (level 3), player-pursuing Chasers (level 4), multi-HP Tanks (level 5), and wall-phasing Ghosts (level 7). Includes chain explosions, multiple arena layouts, and explosive visual effects.

Sprites: “Bomb Party” by Matt Hackett, Cem Kalyoncu, /usr/share (CC-BY 3.0) | “Bomberman Explosion Effect” by Satik64 (CC0)

Bomberman - Free Online Arcade Bomb Game

Play Bomberman free in your browser -- navigate grid-based arenas, place bombs to destroy walls and defeat enemies, collect power-ups, and advance through increasingly challenging levels with explosive visual effects.

Features

  • Grid-based arena with indestructible walls, destructible bricks, and walkable tiles
  • Bomb placement with timed fuse and cardinal direction explosions
  • Chain explosions -- bombs detonate adjacent bombs for cascading blasts
  • 5 power-up types: Extra Bombs, Bigger Blast, Speed Boost, Kick, Remote Detonation
  • 3+ AI enemies with wandering movement behavior
  • Multiple arena layouts via procedural level generator with increasing difficulty
  • Lives system with respawn and game over
  • Particle effects, screen shake, fire glow, and floating score text
  • High score persistence and tutorial overlay
