About Space Farming
Space Farming

Casual farm simulation on a space station. Grow 10 crop types through growth stages, including Void Mushrooms, Plasma Peppers, and Astral Flowers with unique weather affinities. Tend 4 livestock types, survive space weather events with enhanced visual effects, and purchase upgrades from the upgrade shop (growth boost, yield multiplier, weather shield, auto-harvester). Sell produce for credits.

Part of the »SynthelicZ« Desktop.
