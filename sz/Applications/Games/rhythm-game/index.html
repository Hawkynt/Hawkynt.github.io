About Rhythm Game
Rhythm Game

Hit falling notes in time with music! Use D/F/J/K keys to hit notes as they reach the hit zone. Timing accuracy determines your score: Perfect, Great, Good, or Miss. Build combos for score multipliers. Features 5+ songs, final letter grades (S/A/B/C/D), burst particles, streak glow, and smooth animations.

Part of the »SynthelicZ« Desktop.
