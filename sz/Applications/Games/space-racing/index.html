About Space Racing
Space Racing

High-speed top-down spaceship racing with boost pads, hazards, AI opponents, and multiple tracks. Choose from 5 unique ships with different stats, earn credits from races, and upgrade your ship's engine, thrusters, gyroscope, and nitro tank. Features speed-line effects, boost flame trails, collision sparks, smooth camera following, and confetti celebrations.

Part of the »SynthelicZ« Desktop.
