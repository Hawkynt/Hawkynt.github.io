About Fantasy Puzzle
Fantasy Puzzle

Manipulate the four elements — fire, water, earth, and air — to solve magical puzzles. Fire burns wood obstacles, water fills channels, earth creates barriers, and air pushes objects. Decipher ancient runes hidden in 15+ progressively challenging levels. Earn star ratings for efficiency. Features elemental particle effects, rune glow, and cascading solution animations.

Fantasy Puzzle - Free Online Elemental Magic Puzzle Game

Cast fire, water, earth, and air spells on a grid to burn wood, fill channels, create barriers, and push blocks. Solve 17 progressively challenging magical puzzle levels with hidden rune discovery and star ratings.

Features

  • Four elements: Fire burns wood, Water fills channels, Earth creates walls, Air pushes blocks
  • 17 puzzle levels with increasing complexity and multi-element combinations
  • Hidden rune discovery system with sparkle effects and auto-reveal on completion
  • Star rating system based on move efficiency (1-3 stars per level)
  • Element-specific particle effects: flame bursts, water ripples, earth crumbles, wind gusts
  • Interactive tutorial overlay and level hints
  • Level map for replaying unlocked levels
  • Persistent progress and high scores via localStorage
