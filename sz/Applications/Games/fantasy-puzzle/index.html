About Fantasy Puzzle
Fantasy Puzzle

Manipulate the four elements — fire, water, earth, and air — to solve magical puzzles. Fire burns wood obstacles, water fills channels, earth creates barriers, and air pushes objects. Decipher ancient runes hidden in 15+ progressively challenging levels. Earn star ratings for efficiency. Features elemental particle effects, rune glow, and cascading solution animations.
