About Physics Puzzle
Physics Puzzle

Aim and launch projectiles using angle and power control to hit goal targets. Place ramps, blocks, and springs to guide your shots. Gravity and momentum simulation drives realistic physics. Earn 1-3 stars based on shot efficiency across 15+ levels. Features collision spark effects, glow trails, structure collapse dust, and 3-star confetti.
