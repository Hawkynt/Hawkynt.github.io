About Asteroids
Asteroids – Enhanced Edition

Classic arcade space shooter with modern visuals, powerups, progressive difficulty, and multiple game modes.

Destroy asteroids and enemy ships while collecting powerups. Build combos for score multipliers. Face 7 rock types with unique death effects, 6 enemy ship types including bosses, and recruit ally fighters as you advance. Choose from Classic, Survival, Zen, or Hardcore modes with selectable field sizes.

Part of the »SynthelicZ« Desktop.
