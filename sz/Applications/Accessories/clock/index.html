About Date and Time

Date and Time - SZ Desktop Clock

A multi-function clock application with five tabs providing analog and digital clock display, alarm management, precision stopwatch, countdown timer, and world clock with interactive map. Part of the SynthelicZ browser-based desktop environment.

Features

  • Analog clock face with smooth-updating hour, minute, and second hands
  • Digital time display and navigable monthly calendar with week numbers
  • Alarm management with configurable repeat modes: Once, Daily, Weekdays
  • Precision stopwatch with millisecond accuracy and lap recording
  • Countdown timer with hours, minutes, seconds input and visual progress bar
  • World clock supporting 22 worldwide time zones with mini analog clocks
  • Interactive world map with day/night terminator and city markers
  • Persistent alarms and world clocks via localStorage
