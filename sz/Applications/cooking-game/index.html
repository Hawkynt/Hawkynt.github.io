About Cooking Game
Cooking Game

Run your own restaurant! Cook 5+ dishes with timing-based minigames, serve customers before their patience runs out, earn tips and star ratings. Upgrade your kitchen between days. Features sizzle/steam particles, perfect-cook glow, burn screen-shake, and smooth cooking animations.
