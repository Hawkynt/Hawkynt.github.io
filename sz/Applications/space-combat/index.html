About Space Combat
Space Combat

Pilot your ship through waves of enemies in intense top-down dogfights. Collect weapon pickups and persistent upgrades to power up your ship. Build kill streaks for massive score multipliers.

Features 8-directional movement, 5 weapon types (spread, laser, missile, plasma, beam), 5 persistent upgrade categories (extra shots, damage, speed, fire rate, shield capacity), 7 enemy types with distinct AI (scouts, fighters, darts, bombers, snipers, carriers that spawn minions, and juggernauts), wave-based progression, shield system, and explosive visual effects.

Part of the »SynthelicZ« Desktop.
