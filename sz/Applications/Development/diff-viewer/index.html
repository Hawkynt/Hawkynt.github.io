About Diff Viewer

Diff Viewer - Online Text Comparison Tool

Compare two texts side by side with highlighting of additions, deletions, and modifications. Supports side-by-side, unified, and inline diff views with options to ignore whitespace, case, and line endings. Navigate between differences and perform word-level diff analysis.

Features

  • Side-by-side, unified, and inline diff view modes
  • Word-level diff highlighting for detailed comparison
  • Options to ignore whitespace, case, and line endings
  • Navigate between differences with previous/next buttons
  • Open files from disk for comparison
  • Swap left and right sides
  • Difference statistics showing added, removed, and changed lines
  • Resizable split panels with drag handle
